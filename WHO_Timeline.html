2025: The Breakthrough and the Beginning

January 2025
Dr. Viktor Orlovich Announces Necronyl

Russian scientist Dr. Viktor Orlovich reveals a revolutionary cancer cure named Necronyl, which reprograms the immune system to regenerate damaged cells rapidly, promising healing and extended lifespan.

February - March 2025
Global Distribution & Early Miracles

Pharmaceutical giants fast-track production. Despite minimal clinical trials, public demand and political pressure push Necronyl into global distribution. Terminal cancer patients show rapid recovery, hailed as a miracle drug.

April 2025
Emergence of Side Effects

Reports arise of insomnia, paranoia, and violent behavior in Necronyl-treated patients. Hospitals document “hyper-healing,” where wounds produce grotesque excess tissue. Early warning signs of something sinister emerge.

May 2025
First Undead Cases Detected

Patients enter death-like stillness and subsequently awaken exhibiting primal aggression, dubbed “undead.” Infection spreads via bites and blood, with regenerative abilities making containment difficult.

2025 - 2026: Collapse and Global Spread

June 2025
Necronyl Banned — Too Late

Authorities ban Necronyl after widespread infection confirmed. Millions already exposed. Undead overwhelm hospitals and cities worldwide, rapidly spreading beyond containment efforts.

July - September 2025
Government Collapse & Urban Fall

Governments falter as military forces are overwhelmed. Entire metropolitan areas fall in days. Refugees flood rural areas, but infection follows. Communication and public services collapse globally.

October 2025 - Early 2026
Scientific Race & Deadly Challenges

Scientists scramble for a cure, hindered by the undead’s rapid regeneration. Fire becomes a primary defense, but scorched earth tactics threaten remaining human refuges. Survival becomes a constant struggle.

2026 - 2027: The New Dark Age

Mid 2026
Humanity Scattered

Only small isolated communities remain, living underground or in heavily fortified sanctuaries. Nights are haunted by undead moans. Survivors adapt harsh new norms in a world without centralized order or hope.

Early 2027
Rumors of the Final Bastion

Whispers circulate about a hidden fortress in Siberia—dubbed the “Final Bastion”—where Dr. Orlovich is rumored to have fled. Some claim he works on a cure, others fear he has become undead himself. Expeditions end in disappearance.

Mid 2027
Current Status

The world remains fractured and dangerous. Communication networks are defunct, global governance is nonexistent, and survival depends on vigilance, community, and often luck. The undead threat persists as the dominant force on the planet.
